All filters are optional and combine: pick a Lane to load just that lane's data (one shard), then narrow by VVD / Revenue Month / special flag. If you enter a VVD or pick a month without a Lane, all lanes are loaded (a few MB) and filtered. Tick "By Target Port" to keep one row per container.
